About Rajasthan Technical University

Discover the history, academics, and vision of Rajasthan Technical University, the premier technical university in Rajasthan.

Overview

Rajasthan Technical University (RTU) is a premier affiliating university located on Rawatbhata Road, Kota, Rajasthan. Established in 2006 by the Government of Rajasthan, RTU was created to enhance the technical education landscape in the state and develop highly skilled engineering professionals. The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and its programs are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

The university currently affiliates over 100 engineering colleges, multiple B.Arch colleges, and various MCA and MBA institutions across Rajasthan, ensuring high-quality education, uniform academic standards, and rigorous evaluation processes for thousands of students.

Academics & Programs

RTU offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, primarily focusing on Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech), Master of Technology (M.Tech), Master of Business Administration (MBA), and Master of Computer Applications (MCA). It follows a comprehensive curriculum designed to meet modern industry requirements.

Departments & Engineering Branches

RTU and its affiliated institutions house a diverse array of specialized departments catering to both core engineering fields and modern technological domains. Students can choose from a multitude of B.Tech and M.Tech branches tailored to their career aspirations.

  • Computer Science & IT: Including specializations in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Machine Learning, and Cyber Security.
  • Core Engineering: Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE).
  • Specialized Branches: Aeronautical Engineering, Petroleum Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Production & Industrial Engineering, and Mechatronics.
  • Management & Applied Sciences: Dedicated departments offering comprehensive MBA and MCA programs.

Each department is equipped with modern laboratories, highly experienced faculty, and active student chapters that encourage technical innovation, research, and practical learning.

Affiliated Colleges & Admissions

RTU serves as the central affiliating body for technical institutions in the state. Admissions to undergraduate engineering programs (B.Tech) in RTU-affiliated colleges are primarily conducted through the Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP). Admissions are generally based on JEE Main scores and Class 12 board examination results, offering a transparent and merit-based entry for aspiring engineers.

RTU Syllabus, Examinations & Results

The university follows a structured semester-based credit system with examinations held twice a year (odd and even semesters). Students can easily access the updated RTU B.Tech Syllabus for all major branches including Computer Science, Civil, Mechanical, Electronics, and Electrical Engineering.

RTU is known for its timely evaluation process. Students can check their latest semester RTU Results and academic notifications directly through the official portals, keeping them informed about their academic progress.

Campus & Facilities

The main RTU campus spreads over a lush, vast area of 385 acres. It provides state-of-the-art infrastructure, including advanced engineering laboratories, a comprehensive central library, modern sports facilities, and well-equipped hostels. The campus environment is designed to foster a productive, innovative, and engaging learning experience for all its students and faculty.

Training & Placement

Recognizing the importance of career readiness, Rajasthan Technical University places a strong emphasis on industry-institute interaction. The dedicated Training and Placement Cell organizes campus recruitment drives, internships, and skill-development workshops. Graduates from RTU Kota and its affiliated colleges are actively recruited by top multinational IT corporations, core engineering firms, and management enterprises globally.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Rajasthan Technical University (RTU) a government university?

Yes, RTU is a state government university established by the Government of Rajasthan in 2006 to enhance technical education in the state.

How can I get admission into RTU for B.Tech?

Admissions to B.Tech programs in RTU-affiliated colleges are primarily conducted through the Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) based on JEE Main scores and Class 12 board examination marks.

How many colleges are affiliated with RTU Kota?

RTU serves as the affiliating body for over 100 technical institutions, including engineering (B.Tech/M.Tech), architecture (B.Arch), and management (MBA/MCA) colleges across Rajasthan.
