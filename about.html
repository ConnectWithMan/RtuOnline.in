Your trusted companion for Rajasthan Technical University academic resources.

Our Mission

At RTUOnline.in, our primary mission is to simplify the academic journey for B.Tech students enrolled under Rajasthan Technical University. We understand the challenges students face when looking for reliable, organized, and easily accessible study materials.

We provide a comprehensive collection of Previous Year Question Papers (PYQ) to help you prepare effectively for your semester exams, all in one easy-to-use platform.

What We Offer

We carefully curate and categorize past examination papers across various branches including 1st Year (Common), Computer Science (and related branches), Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Our platform allows you to find your required papers seamlessly and utilize them for your offline study.
