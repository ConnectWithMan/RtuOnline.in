Frequently Asked Questions about RTU Result

How can I check my RTU Result?

You can check your RTU result by selecting your course on this page and clicking on the relevant semester link. You will be redirected to the official RTU portal where you need to enter your roll number.

What details are required to check the RTU Result?

To view your RTU result, you generally need your examination Roll Number, Name, and Mother's Name as per your university records.
