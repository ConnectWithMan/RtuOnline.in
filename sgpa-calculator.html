About the RTU SGPA Calculator

The RTU SGPA Calculator is a simple online tool for Rajasthan Technical University students who want to estimate their Semester Grade Point Average. It uses each subject's credit value and earned grade to calculate a credit-weighted SGPA.

You can use this RTU SGPA calculator for any semester by entering the subjects shown on your grade card. The calculator supports grades from A++ to F and displays the estimated result to two decimal places, making it easy to review your semester performance without calculating every credit-point product manually.

For course and exam preparation, explore the RTU syllabus and RTU previous year papers. After calculating SGPA for multiple semesters, use the RTU CGPA Calculator to estimate your cumulative grade point average.

How to Use the RTU SGPA Calculator

  1. Select the number of subjects listed on your grade card.
  2. Enter each subject name, its credit value and the grade you received.
  3. Select Calculate SGPA to see your estimated semester grade point average.

RTU SGPA Formula

SGPA is a credit-weighted average. Multiply the credits for every subject by the corresponding grade point, add those results and divide by the total credits.

SGPA = Sum of (Subject Credit x Grade Point) / Total Credits

Example: If three subjects have credit-point products of 30, 36 and 24, the weighted total is 90. If their combined credits are 11, the estimated SGPA is 90 / 11 = 8.18.

RTU SGPA Calculator FAQs

What is SGPA in RTU?

SGPA means Semester Grade Point Average. It represents your credit-weighted academic performance for one semester.

How is RTU SGPA calculated?

Multiply each subject's credits by its grade point, add the weighted grade points and divide by the total credits counted in the semester.

Is this RTU SGPA Calculator official?

No. It is an independent estimation tool. Use your official RTU grade card or university rules for final academic records.
