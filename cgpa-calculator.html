About the RTU CGPA Calculator

The RTU CGPA Calculator helps Rajasthan Technical University students estimate their Cumulative Grade Point Average across multiple semesters. It gives each semester the correct weight by using both the semester SGPA and the credits completed in that term.

Enter the values shown on your grade cards and the calculator will display the estimated CGPA to two decimal places. If you still need a semester result, first use the RTU SGPA Calculator. You can also explore the RTU syllabus and RTU previous year papers.

How to Use the RTU CGPA Calculator

  1. Select how many semesters you want to include.
  2. Enter the SGPA and total credits for each semester from your grade cards.
  3. Select Calculate CGPA to view your credit-weighted cumulative average.

RTU CGPA Formula

Multiply each semester SGPA by the credits completed in that semester. Add all weighted values, then divide by the combined credits for the included semesters.

CGPA = Sum of (Semester SGPA x Semester Credits) / Total Credits

Example: If Semester 1 has an SGPA of 8.00 with 20 credits and Semester 2 has an SGPA of 9.00 with 24 credits, the estimated CGPA is (8.00 x 20 + 9.00 x 24) / 44 = 8.55.

RTU CGPA Calculator FAQs

What is CGPA in RTU?

CGPA means Cumulative Grade Point Average. It represents your credit-weighted academic performance across multiple semesters.

How is RTU CGPA calculated?

Multiply every semester SGPA by its semester credits, add the weighted values and divide by the total credits across all included semesters.

Is this RTU CGPA Calculator official?

No. It is an independent estimation tool. Use your official RTU grade cards and applicable university rules for final academic records.
